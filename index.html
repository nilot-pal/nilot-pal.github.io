I am a 2nd year Phd student at the Department of Engineering Science and Mechanics (ESM) at Virginia Tech. Previously, I was a software developer for laser propagation system at Zeus Numerix, Pune. I obtained my BS in Mechanical Engineering from National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirappali (NITT). My interests are scientific machine learning and data science, stochastic processes, optimization and high performance computing with applications to engineering, . An updated list of publications can be found on my Google Scholar profile and some of my software contributions can be found on Github. If you're interested in a high-level overview of some of my research, check out these recordings of recent talks [1], [2], [3], [4].
